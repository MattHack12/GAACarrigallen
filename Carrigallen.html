Welcome to Carrigallen GAA

Discover the heart and spirit of Gaelic football in Carrigallen. From thrilling matches to community events, we bring people together through sport and tradition.

About Us

Founded in 1889, Carrigallen GAA is a cornerstone of the local community. We are proud of our heritage, our players, and our fans who make the club what it is today.

Our mission is to foster talent, promote sportsmanship, and instill a lifelong love for Gaelic games in players of all ages.
